About Techsurge & Mridang

Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University is a premier institute in the field of technology. The Institute is established by Maharaja Agrasen Technical Education Society promoted by gropu og well known Industrialists, Businessmen, Professionals and Philanthropists with an aim to promote quality education in the field of Technology.


The knowledge, skill and acumen instilled in the students has been instrumenal in creating prospective leaders for tomorrow and making them stand above the hoi polloi.

KEEPERS CODERS FINDERS

DESCRIPTION

In this event participants will come in teams of two. A single code is divided into three parts. Each part will be coded in every round with the help of clues. Each team will be given the same set of clues which will include aptitude and logical reasoning questions. This will occur in rounds, first round will be of 15mins, second round will be of 25 mins and third round will be of 35 mins. In each round the team has to solve the clue i.e. aptitude or logical reasoning question and do the coding, the team which is unable to do so will be eliminated round-wise. The team which is able to do the correct coding in the least time wins.

CODING JUNCTION 2k18

DESCRIPTION

This is a full day event with every round testing a different skills and thinking of the participant. TECHSURGE and MRIDANG in association with CODEMATRIX (Coding society of MAIT) presents to you CODING JUNCTION-2k18, technical event to show your coding skills to everyone.Coding junction is one of the best coding competition of techsurge. It facilitates with all round development of programmers. This event comprises of three rounds. Event is divided into 3 parts Round-1 (Basic computer science knowledge) Coding and logic based 60 question will be given and time allotted to solve these questions is 60 minutes.it is open for all at no cost the pattern of the questions of this round will be single correct MCQs and multi correct MCQs.This round has two sections, first one contain 35 question of basic knowledge about C, C++, HTML, CSS, Data Structure, ADA, etc. and the second section have 25 question about coding aptitude. Round-2 (Code Sprint) 7 problems with variable difficulty will be given and participants will code their solutions. Round-3 (Reverse Coding) You will be been given outputs for various input values, you just have to write the code for it. It must have to satisfy some test cases.
